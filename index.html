Professional Landscape Services

Gordon Gardening Solutions has been serving Denver homeowners and businesses since 2005, providing comprehensive landscape design and maintenance services. Our team of certified horticulturists specializes in creating sustainable outdoor spaces that thrive in Colorado's unique climate. From xeriscaping to perennial garden installations, we combine environmental consciousness with aesthetic design principles. Our services include soil analysis, irrigation system installation, and native plant selection. We adhere to water conservation guidelines while maintaining the natural beauty of your property. Each project begins with a consultation to understand your vision and assess your property's specific needs.

Seasonal Lawn Care Programs

Our year-round lawn maintenance program ensures your turf remains healthy through Denver's varying seasons. We offer aeration, fertilization, weed control, and organic pest management services tailored to local grass varieties. Our experts monitor soil pH levels and provide core aeration services to improve oxygen circulation. Winter preparation services include snow mold prevention and protective mulching. Summer care focuses on proper watering techniques and heat stress management. All treatments use EPA-approved products applied by licensed technicians. We maintain detailed service records for each client and provide seasonal care recommendations.

Sustainable Gardening Practices

At Gordon Gardening Solutions, we prioritize eco-friendly methods that reduce environmental impact. Our composting program recycles yard waste into nutrient-rich soil amendments. We implement water-wise irrigation systems with smart controllers that adjust to weather conditions. Our pollinator garden installations help support local ecosystems while adding vibrant color to your landscape. We specialize in drought-resistant plant varieties that require minimal watering once established. Our team stays current with Colorado State University Extension research to provide science-based solutions. We offer educational workshops on sustainable gardening techniques for interested clients.
